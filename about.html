The accurate Movies and Series Recommendation system

In this hustling world, entertainment is a necessity for each one of us to refresh our mood and energy. Entertainment regains our confidence for work and we can work more enthusiastically.
For revitalizing ourselves, we can listen to our preferred music or can watch movies of our choice. For watching favourable movies online we can utilize movie recommendation systems, which are more reliable, since searching for preferred movies will require more and more time which one cannot afford to waste and there is no guarantee that a particular random choice of the movie will be to our taste and the probability of us liking that too is therefore random and on some cases might be very small.
In this paper, to improve the quality of a movie recommendation system, a Hybrid approach is discussed which is done by combining the content-based filtering which focuses on the description, cast and other describing parameters of the movie with the collaborative filtering system which focuses on the similarity with other users, using Support Vector Machine as a classifier and genetic algorithm is presented in the proposed methodology and comparative results have been shown which depicts that the proposed approach shows an improvement in the accuracy, quality and scalability of the movie recommendation system than the pure approaches in three different datasets. The hybrid approach helps to get the advantages from both approaches as well as tries to eliminate the drawbacks of both methods, which include the problem of a cold start or that of recommending a new movie with no initial ratings.
The approach has been fruitful and, in our work, we recommend movies by taking a minimum of three parameters which are movies, we ask them in a certain order to satisfy our algorithm and after which the recommendations are based on our correlation with the other users and also on the closeness with the descriptions of our movie with the ones in the dataset.
The model gives a fast and accurate result to the users and is also integrated with a User Interface and hosted online. We present, MRScanners, a dynamic movie-recommendation system available as a web and executable application!
